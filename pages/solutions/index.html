How to elevate a door’s integrity?

Retone has facilitated the following solutions to elevate the integrity of anti-theft doors: 1. The incorporation of steel bars and multiple metal rods of various metals which intertwine into a grid form. This grid is integrated into the Hero door’s body. It provides an elaborate structural defense against intrusion and penetration. Although, this additional implementation results in a weightier product, it does not impede the transportation or installation procedures of the product. The proprietary solution of Retone takes into consideration and allowance, the disassembly of the door and its various peripheral and inclusive elements.
2. The facilitation of exceptional nanocomposite material in Retone’s proprietary invention, provides various properties which elevate a door’s integrity. The front and rear of the double-sided door are integrated with each other to absorb multiple, potentially destructive shocks which are then distributed evenly through the Hero Retone body. This shock force is then transmitted to the Hero Retone frame section and transmitted to the reinforced concrete behind it.

Preservation of Natural Resources

Retone Hero uses wood and other natural material in order to deliver the sense and appearance of nature in the products' aesthetics; conveying a sense of peace in man's surroundings, which only nature can deliver. How can the least amount of natural resources be harvested while still producing doors that allow man to benefit from nature's bounties? Man's propensity towards the utilization of wood in various home or office decor applications is causing the depletion of this valuable, natural resource and its benefits, available to man. This need is best satisfied by industrial tree planting as opposed to tree cutting of existing trees which only further endangers the earth's depreciating forests. This can save the earth from further damage as well as soil erosion in the most effective way. The goal is to continue to satisfy man's inclination towards nature's grounding and healing properties, found in wood and the mineral kingdom, by bringing them into his interior design; while simultaneously protecting the earth's precious and diminishing natural resources. Retone resists the cutting of trees indiscriminately by inventing new materials and provides optimal facilitation of wood.

Upgrading the Potentials of Door Design

In the process of manufacturing doors, we sometimes come across propositions from designers concerning the facilitation of upgrades in door casing materials. The use of such upgraded materials may require rudimentary adjustments. For example, the use of stone in the door casing causes a significant increase in the door’s weight, which creates new challenges in the door’s structural aspects. Retone has created a sequence of high-tech resolutions to allow the diversification of materials in the door’s visage, increasing the speed and scale of the manufacturing process of doors and integrated elements.

Retone Hero: From Production Site, to End User

Conventional doors are usually obtained from a store or production facility. The client is characteristically responsible for transportation and installation while suppliers are not liable for any damages incurred along the way nor during installation. On the contrary, circumstances may arise where the product and installation slot have size discrepancies; in which case, changes are necessarily made to the installation slot. Retone has devised solutions for both predicaments. Retone takes on responsibility for every supply end phase of the process; from production facility, to use of door by the end user. In addition, before installation of the sub frame, Retone monitoring experts interact with the contractor and match the sub frames to the standard door size.
