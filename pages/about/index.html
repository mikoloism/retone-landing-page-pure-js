About Retone

In order to reduce deforestation and reach the potentials of the door manufacturing industry, the objective of our brand is to fulfill the needs of man while considering the rights of nature.

The founders of Retone in Sweden have replaced conventional methods with unique and modern properties of design, structure and material. The invention of the materials used by Retone, shows the founders' commitment to quality of product and Retone's respect towards nature.

The designs of Retone's doors are inspired by nature, with utmost attention to the accuracy of geometric calculations which results in Retone's doors being manifested as works of art and precision. The stylish designs of our doors harmonizes perfectly with the form and color of exquisitely designed houses.

The variety of styles and designs found in Retone's annual collections are of exemplary standard. The inspiring designs of Retone doors can provide architects with original ideas for form and layout.

Retone is resolutely committed to timely delivery of orders. Retone ensures the presence of experts at each stage of the purchasing process to guaranty meticulous execution of product, and complete customer satisfaction. Retone offers a seamless series of services from company facility to installation.
