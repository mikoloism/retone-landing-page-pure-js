The Journey of Retone

The Merger of Nature's enchantment and Artificial Intelligence's precision

Part I (1)

From inception to creation, Retone has traversed a long road. Our intention was to draw attention to and portray the importance of a door's role in interior design. As such, doors should be considered a decorative and original part in this design aspect, rather than their oft seen role of filling the framework of our shelters. Retone's vision was to create doors with maximum endurance and strength in its perfection of beauty, elegance and harmony.

A variety of choices was at our disposal to visualize those goals. Our catalyst, was interpreting the level of strength and durability that could be envisioned for the safety that our doors would furnish for Retone's clients.

What was the summit that could be achieved beyond current, global, door safety? Our decision was to anchor our ideal at the eye of vision and bellow the ceiling on the existing level of endurance. Due to Retone's idealistic vortex, it was pertinent for Retone to give birth to a new brain child; a door that would be embellished with elite aesthetical values while remaining true to its purpose as a shield and protection against the intrusions of man and nature on our protective structural cocoon.

Part II (2)

Typically, and conventionally, the relationship between a door’s durability and its aesthetics may appear insignificant.  However, Retone’s conceptualization and vision of a door, differs in this regard.  Retone has a holistic approach in its design and creation whereby, all a door’s elements amalgamate, allowing integral interrelation and definition. Our goal was designing a product where a sense of glory and endurance would be instilled into the end user and his community while reflecting subtle artistic values.  These ideals should not distract or detract from the goal of a door’s role in safety, nor from the end user’s peace of mind.  The time to return to the drafting table and innovate was at our proverbial door step. Our mission? To create the deepest, most accessible emotions of our audience with the simplest and most familiar game plan while passing through the “heart of nature”, the origin and abode of man. In addition to the sense of originality that nature imparts, natural surroundings also shout out: safe haven; a sentiment unfound in synthetics. It is not only ancient, but timeless.

Part III (3)

With our feet firmly grounded on mother earth’s ground, in a symbiosis with her, her enchantments and inspiration; our goals were crystal clear. Retone’s doors would achieve the highest standard of elegance lent to us by shaking mother earth’s hands with modern know how, resulting in captivating enchantment, and still achieving the goal of respecting and saving mother nature, for our future generations because our doors have been created with recycling and sustainability in mind. We have all your nature preservation concerns, covered.

Armed with our polished considerations and intentions; what was to follow was an entourage of criteria that would naturally lead to a philosophy; a philosophy that was required to create Retone’s identity. This identity was shaping itself by reassessing and redefining the concept of a door, and had hit a distinct perspective, in sight of a fusion of scientific, artistic, philosophical, moral, and technological strands of creation. The ensuing approach articulated all our values, our vision as well as our policies in a coherent and systematic way and would lead us to the general atmosphere of all constituents of commissioning a project; from design to product line.

Part IV (4)

Conceptualizing products’ blueprints where the hallmark of taste, blends seamlessly with a summit of functional ease, we assumed an obligation of facilitating maximum coordination between varying and multitudinous facets of product design. An Artist’s vision and intuition, natural material discernment and its refinement, product concept modeling as well as an engineer’s computations of product structure and form; all ­­­called for the guidance and consensus of a host of masters in respective disciplines of art and industry.

Part V (5)

Our approach to product design called for an adherence to minimalist, aesthetic principles and the elimination of optical, excessiveness. Brand identity in addition, also called for golden ratios and grid structures as requisites of mathematical and geometrically precise design, depicting the modern era.

Converging minimalist thinking with the precision of an engineer’s skill, delivered simple, clean and flawless products; pertinent aesthetic solutions for the onslaught of stresses created by the complexities of the contemporary world.

In product design, the potential for calibrating the correlation between aesthetics and human implementation, so that attractiveness of design follows function, must be given its due consideration. All human comforts derived from allowance of relaxation, ergonomics, luxury and ease of serviceability were among the core components of our identity and high on the list of our tangible values, ascertaining further ease and familiarity. Our doors should yield all levels of end users the capacity to undergo commensurate sensual and subconscious transposition of the brush of a delicate breeze in a temperate forest.

Part VI (6)

Additionally, a holistic and coherent proposition would be vital, if it was to incorporate in its entirety, the diverse values of technology, science, philosophy, art and brand identity where all phenomena coagulate like the casserole of a seasoned chef.

Part VII (7)

Besides this, a product line able to simultaneously converge the values from philosophy, art, science and technology while defining a brand identity was going to require a holistic and coherent vision whereby all phenomena submitting to a brand identity and essential values, interact in harmony. They are orchestrated in concert with each other, supporting and reforming one another as they march forward reflecting an agreement of values. Articulation of this coherence and coordination was the thread that determined the success of the output product line design.

Part VIII (8)

The basic values of the brand were instrumental in creating the special production line of the doors. Human beings are an innovative element meeting the creative aspects of door production.

The construction of the special production line of these doors was a function of the basic values of the brand. In this line of production, human beings are considered as an innovative element that meets the creative aspects of the production of the work. Heavy parts are transported in the production line by making carrier ligaments.

Ensuing human creativity, technology was the cardinal element of our product line. Merging the fluidity and expressiveness of human creativity with the precision of artificial intelligence would enable the creation of the quintessential artistic invention; original and fluid in its artistic expression and visionary. We built our industrial platform in line with fourth generation technology approaches making, the components of the production line optimally designed by artificial intelligence for the optimum and most efficient use of resources, acknowledging the limitations of human mind. Data is recorded continuously and accurately, in each component of the process, resulting in an incremental, and continual, increase of learning. Also, most of the intelligent transferal and communication between the different robots of this production line should have taken place. Our rationale for selecting the most advanced technology in our production line, allowing the best to cover our brand identity, creating a transcendent product; efficient and estimable.
